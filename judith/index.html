// what she is

Not a chatbot in a tab.
A broadcast desk that lives on my hardware.

Judith is the anchor of a private newsroom. You speak; she transcribes, reasons over your world — news, markets, and the state of your own projects — then speaks back and reconstructs the answer on screen. Her reply splits into prose she narrates and exhibit cards that ignite as she talks, the way a live broadcast cuts to graphics. No cloud account, no API tab — the whole brain runs on machines I own.
