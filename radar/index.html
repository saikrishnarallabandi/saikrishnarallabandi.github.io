Research notes on technology and the systems shaping our future.

Rallabandi Radar tracks technologies moving from demo to deployment — humanoid robotics, AI agents, and the infrastructure assembling underneath both — and asks what they do to the arrangements people already live inside.

The recurring method is comparison: a Venetian trade charter next to a robotics supply chain, black hole thermodynamics next to a model's loss surface. Old systems are the only long-run data we have on new ones.
