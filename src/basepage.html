The 'De-Entanglement' Framework

My doctoral research introduced a foundational framework to build more robust, transparent, and ubiquitous speech technologies. It works by isolating the relevant causal factors of variation in data, conceptualizing speech as two "first-class objects": **Content** (the 'what') and **Style** (the 'how').
